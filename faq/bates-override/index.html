Can I override the Bates Ledger if I want to start at 1?

Category: Bates Stamping • Last Updated May 2026

**Yes, absolutely.** While the automatic ledger prevents accidental duplicates, we understand that certain cases require starting multiple, separate volumes at number `1` using the same prefix (e.g., establishing sub-categories or working on distinct court branches).

How to execute a manual override:

  1. Type your desired text into the **Bates Prefix** box.
  2. Observe the **Start Index** field auto-populate with the next number from history.
  3. Simply **click inside the Start Index** box.
  4. Delete the auto-filled number and **type whatever number you desire** (e.g., `1`).
  5. Click "Execute Merge."

The software will always defer to your **literal manual text inputs** over its internal ledger database when applying physical stamps.

⚠️ Note: When your override completes, the ledger database will automatically update itself to record the *new* highest-ever index reached, maintaining accurate tracking for your next subsequent merge!
