Where is my defensible Merge Audit Log?

Category: File Management • Last Updated May 2026

Access Paralegal Suite values defensibility. Every single compilation creates a permanent, secure ledger receipt in the form of a **`Merge_Audit_Log.txt`** file.

Location of the Log

The audit log generates dynamically inside the **exact same folder** you designated for your output PDF. For example:

  • If your output is: `C:\Cases\Smith_Master_Bundle.pdf`
  • Your audit log will be: `C:\Cases\Merge_Audit_Log.txt`

Furthermore, upon a successful merge completion, the software automatically triggers your default system text editor to **instantly pop-open the log**, meaning you don't even need to search your hard drive for it.

What information is inside?

  • Timestamp of compilation
  • The exact visual index order sequence
  • Original filenames processed
  • Assigned Bates serialization spans (e.g., Exhibit A: 001 - 015)
⚖️ Admissibility Tip: Keep the `Merge_Audit_Log.txt` archived alongside your master PDF. If the opposing counsel ever challenges the integrity or ordering of the bundle, you have an instant machine-generated defensible receipt verifying exact original file mappings!
