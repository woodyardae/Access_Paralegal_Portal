I reordered my PDFs in the queue. How does the software know the right order?

Category: Operation Instructions • Last Updated May 2026

Access Paralegal Suite uses a visual **Literal Sequence Engine**. It completely ignores the original names, timestamps, or alphabetical order stored on your computer disk.

Instead, when you click "Execute Merge," our software executes a **Top-to-Bottom visual scan** of the queue list widget in your window.

How it works:

Whatever file is visual row **#1** becomes Page 1 of your merged output bundle. The file in row **#2** is appended directly afterward, and so on.

💡 Verification Tip: Before clicking merge, take a quick look at the '#' numbers column on the left of the file list. They represent the absolute sequential compilation order. If that looks right, your final document will be perfectly assembled.
