Do I need an internet connection to use this software?

Category: Security & Compliance • Last Updated May 2026

Absolutely not. Access Paralegal Suite was built from the ground up following an "Offline-First Security Sandbox" posture. Once the software is downloaded and activated, it requires **zero** internet connection to compile, merge, or stamp your documents.

Your sensitive legal case work never leaves your local drive. Unlike cloud-based subscription services that upload your client documents to their servers (introducing severe privacy, subpoena, and hacker attack vectors), all data processing is executed entirely within your computer's RAM and CPU.

🛡️ Enterprise Security Note: If you work in high-compliance environments, you can completely sever your workstation's internet connection while processing sensitive bundles. The system executes 100% identical logic disconnected.

What functions occur locally?

  • Merging 100+ Exhibit PDFs into Master Bundles
  • Email Parsing and Attachment Harvesting
  • AES-256 Case Vault encryption & storage
  • Bates Stamping overlay and page flattening
  • Audit Log hashing and generations
